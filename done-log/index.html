Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Done Log mobile application ("the App") handles user information.

1. Information Collection

The App is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. The App does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

User activity logs (such as timestamps of completed sessions) are stored locally on the user's device. These logs are not transmitted to the developer or any external servers.

2. Local Data Storage

The App stores usage data locally on the user's device to enable features such as:

  • Session logging
  • Daily session counts
  • Streak tracking
  • Session history

This data remains on the user's device unless the user deletes the App or clears the app data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to support functionality such as advertising or analytics. These services may collect information used to identify your device.

Examples of such services include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may collect certain information such as device identifiers to deliver relevant ads.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

The App does not transmit personal data to external servers. All usage data is stored locally on the device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
